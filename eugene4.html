WHO WILL MAKE TOP 4?

Chelsea,Liverpool or Leicester.

During matchweek 37,we saw a repeat of the FA Cup final in which Chelsea excerted revenge by beating the FA Cup winners,Leicester by 2 goals to 1.Early in the first half,Timo Werner had his two goals disallowed by VAR due to offside and a handball.There was also a penalty claim in the first half.Antonio Rudiger opened the scoring at the 49th minute while Jorginho scored a penalty in the 63rd minute after Wesley Fofana tripped Timo Werner in the penalty box.However,substitute Kelechi Ihenacho gave Leicester hopes by pulling a goal back in the 77th minute.However,this comeback wasn't meant to be as Chelsea emerged winners.This took Chelsea up to third while Leicester dropped down to fifth.Meanhile at Turf Moor,Liverpool beat Burnley by three goals to nil.Goals from Roberto Firmino,Nathaniel Philips and Oxlade-Chamberlain sealed all three points pushing Liverpool to fourth place ahead of Leicester by goal difference.This setsup an interesting final day fixture as Chelsea face Aston Villa,whole sealed a comeback victory against Spurs,at Villa Park while Liverpool face Crystal Palace at Anfield and Leicester City faces Totenham Hotspur at the King Powe Stadium.Victories from Chelsea and Liverpool will seal a Champions League spot,however,Liverpool must ensure they have a higher goal difference than Leicester for if Leicester wins by a higher goal difference it would guarantee them a Champions League position.
