WACHIRA NEWSPAPER ENTERPRISES

Saturday 15 May 2021

3.00 pm-5.00 pm

The match started just like any other match you know players shaking hands and the crowd cheering for their team and jeering for the opposite team.It was the famous derby, which was known very well by the Kilikoni residence,it was Sungura FC against Kamusinga FC. Kamusinga FC opened the scoring at the stroke of halftime.However in the second half,Sungura FC found their scoring boots for they came from behind and won the game 3-1. Goals from Kevin Omondi and Kalvin Ouma who scored a brace at the 65th,75th and 90th minute respectively ensured Sungura FC grabbed all three points.
